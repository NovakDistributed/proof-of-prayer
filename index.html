Proof of Prayer

This web site allows you to automatically, and provably, pray.

Copies of prayers are cryptographically hashed, together with unique information provided by you, to produce a hash value. This hash value, together with the specification of what hashing was done to produce it, can be used by anyone to mathematically verify that the given number of copies of the given prayer were run through the hash function.
